Description:
BigBattery offers a wide range of energy solutions spanning residential, commercial, and industrial use cases. However, the complexity of technical specifications created friction for both first-time buyers and enterprise customers.
This project focused on transforming complex energy systems into clear, scannable, and conversion-optimized visual narratives through high-impact infographics and branded assets.

By combining industrial-grade visual clarity with data-backed iteration, the goal was to:
1. Reduce cognitive load
2. Increase buyer confidence
3. Improve conversion across multiple acquisition channels.

Design Approach:
  • Translated technical specifications into visual storytelling
  • Brand-aligned information architecture for complex energy products
  • Built modular infographic systems that scaled across:
    • Voltage types
    • Use cases
    • Customer segments
  • Optimized layouts for fast scanning, comparison, and decision-making
  • Maintained strong brand consistency across web, ads, and lifecycle emails
  • Continuously refined designs using real user behavior data
